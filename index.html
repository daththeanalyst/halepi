Our Story

The Kazolides Family

Since 1966, the Kazolides family has welcomed guests to Halepi, their authentic Greek & Cypriot taverna bringing the warmth and flavours of the Mediterranean to the heart of London. What began as a humble family restaurant has blossomed into one of the capital's most beloved Greek Cypriot dining experiences, earning recognition from diners around the world.

Steve and Kostas Kazolides have carried on the family tradition since 1984, maintaining the same commitment to authentic, home-cooked recipes passed down through generations. Every dish tells a story of our Mediterranean heritage — from slow-roasted kleftiko to crispy calamari, each plate is prepared with love and the finest ingredients.

Recognised by the Automobile Association of America Guide as one of the finest Greek restaurants in the world, Halepi remains a place where friends become family, and every meal is a celebration.
